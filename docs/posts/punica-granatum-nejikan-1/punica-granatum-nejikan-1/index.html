Punica Granatum Neji-Kan 1

2024/11/15

Neji-kan pomegranate purchased from House of Bonsai in November 2024.

2024 December

Picked this up because I like the trunk. The twisted trunk appearance is one of my favorite styles, looking forward to watching it develop.

I plan to size up the pot with better-draining soil in the spring.

2025 January

Potted up to a ~1gal, very light pruning.

It’s starting to wake up, hopefully not too soon.
