Punica Granatum 6

2024/12/24

Pomegranate (Punica granatum) cutting started in March 2024.

2024 July

Initial growth on this cutting.

2024 December

Trimmed back after it got attacked by bugs. This tree is root bound and seems happy. It experienced the least dieback of all the thick cuttings. In early spring I will re-scar the chop and try to set up better flow into the new leader.

In hindsight I wish this cutting was a bit smaller diameter. I have no idea what kind of healing process to expect but it will be a fun experiment.

2025 January

Repotted and carved. This one had less deadwood to remove so I experimented with making the carving very smooth. I suspect it will look unnatural as it grows out but looking forward to finding out.

I have no clue where this tree will go but the trunk is very straight. Maybe an air layer eventually.
