Biscuits

2024/12/24

Ingredients

  • Flour (2 cups)
  • Granulated sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Baking powder (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Butter (unsalted, cold) (6 tbsp)
  • Whole milk (3/4 cup)

Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 425 F
  2. Add dry ingredients to a bowl and mix
    • Flour (2 cups)
    • Baking powder (1 tbsp)
    • Sugar (1 tbsp)
    • Salt (1 tsp)
  3. Cube butter and mix into the bowl
    • Butter (6 tbsp)
  4. Add milk, mix
    • Milk (3/4 cup)
  5. Roll out on a floured surface, fold, repeat a couple times
  6. Cut out 6-8 biscuits, place on greased baking sheet
  7. Cook until done (~12 minutes)

Notes

  • Don’t overwork the dough
  • Chunks of butter is okay
  • I use a coffee mug to press out circles from the dough
