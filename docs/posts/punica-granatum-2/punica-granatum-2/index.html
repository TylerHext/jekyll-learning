Punica Granatum 2

2024/12/24

Pomegranate (Punica granatum) cutting started in March 2024.

2024 July

Initial growth on this cutting.

2024 December

Depending how the roots look I am planning to chop back to that main curvy branch and use it as the new leader. I’m currently undecided whether I’ll keep any of the other branches.

2024 December

Repotting time.

This was my first attempt planting on top of a plate, the wires ended up messier than I would like but looking forward to seeing how the tree responds.

I got distracted by the root work and didn’t complete the deadwood carving as I had intended. If it grows well this spring I’ll deal with it then, otherwise I’ll wait until this time next year.

I still plan to use the curvy branch as the new leader, probably cutting back to the first note and continuing the trunk from there.

The branch on the inside of the first bend will have to go I think.

I left these little roots to see if they become anything interesting, though I’m not convinced I’ll want them…
