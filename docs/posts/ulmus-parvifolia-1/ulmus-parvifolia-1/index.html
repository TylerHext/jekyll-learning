Ulmus Parvifolia 1

2024/12/24

Chinese elm (Ulmus parvifolia) purchased from an online bonsai outlet store in spring 2024.

2024 May

Earliest photo I have of this tree. It kept most of its foliage from the journey across the country.

2024 June

Strong summer growth. In hindsight I may have been overwatering at this point.

2024 September

In the fall this tree was not doing well, at one point getting black spots and dropping all of its leaves. It bounced back with some growth shortly after this photo. Looking forward to getting it into better soil soon.

2024 December

Seems dormant finally. I’m starting to consider pruning options for spring. The branches are overcrowded and I’d like to increase the overall height of the tree.
